## Sales Dashboard – Supermarket Shop (Power BI Project)
This Power BI project analyzes supermarket sales performance across products, categories, time periods, sales types, and payment modes.
The dashboard provides a quick and clear view of overall business health and daily operational trends.

# Objective
To build an interactive dashboard that:
•	Tracks total sales, total profit, and profit percentage
•	Identifies top-performing products and categories
•	Analyzes monthly and daily sales patterns
•	Compares sales type (Direct / Online) and payment modes (Cash / Online)
•	Helps business users quickly monitor performance and make decisions

# Dataset Summary
The dataset includes:
•	Product-level sales
•	Date fields (daily + monthly)
•	Categories & product hierarchy
•	Profit and selling value
•	Payment modes
•	Sales types (Direct / Online)

# Process
Data Cleaning
•	Validated dates
•	Standardized product and category names
•	Ensured consistent numeric formatting

Modeling
•	Single-table model optimized for filtering
•	DAX calculations for:
o	Total Sales
o	Total Profit
o	Profit %
o	Category & product ranking

Dashboard Design
•	KPI cards for quick summaries
•	Monthly & daily trend visuals
•	Bar charts for product performance
•	Donut charts for sales type & payment mode
•	Treemap for category contribution
•	Filters for Year, Month, Sales Type, and Payment Mode

# Conclusion
This dashboard provides a clear and actionable overview of supermarket sales performance.
It highlights key revenue drivers, product strengths, seasonal trends, and customer purchasing behavior—making it a valuable tool for day-to-day business decision-making.
